# Tally Prime Clone - Complete Accounting ERP

## Vision
A comprehensive keyboard-driven accounting and inventory management system replicating Tally Prime's functionality and workflow. Built for accountants, bookkeepers, and business owners who need professional-grade financial management with GST compliance, multi-company support, and complete statutory reporting.

## Design
Classic business software aesthetic - utilitarian, keyboard-first, data-dense.

Colors (HSL):
--primary: 221 83% 33% (deep blue #1E3A8A) - headers, primary actions
--accent: 25 95% 53% (orange #F97316) - highlights, active states
--background: 220 14% 96% (light gray #F3F4F6) - panels
--foreground: 222 47% 11% (near black) - text
--muted: 220 13% 91% (medium gray) - borders, inactive
--card: 0 0% 100% (pure white) - work areas

Fonts:
Headings: IBM Plex Sans (600/700)
Body: IBM Plex Sans (400/500)
Data: IBM Plex Mono (400/500)

Style: Dense grid layouts, function-key shortcuts, keyboard navigation, minimal chrome

## Features
1. Gateway Menu - Keyboard-driven main navigation (F1-F12 shortcuts)
2. Company Management - Multi-company, financial years, backup/restore
3. Chart of Accounts - Groups, ledgers, opening balances, GST tags
4. Voucher System - All types (Contra, Payment, Receipt, Journal, Sales, Purchase, Credit/Debit Note)
5. Inventory - Items, units, stock groups, godowns, stock valuation
6. GST Compliance - GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, e-invoice, e-way bill
7. Banking - Reconciliation, cheque printing, payment advice
8. Reports - Trial Balance, P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, Stock Summary, Day Book
9. User Management - Role-based access, audit trail
10. Taxation - TDS, TCS calculations
